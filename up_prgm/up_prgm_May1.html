VOICES IN FLIGHT

Soprano: Xingtian Ge | Pianist: Yizhe Lin
Friday, 1 May 2026, 1:00 PM
Bournville Quaker Meeting House,
65 Linden Road, Birmingham, B30 1JT
Voices in Flight is a journey into the air, carried by the human voice. In the world of vocal music, the coloratura soprano is the voice closest to flight. This sound shimmers and soars beyond the limits of range, unfolding brilliant cascades of notes high above. The agile runs and crystalline high tones are not merely feats of virtuosity, but expressions of emotional freedom. Join us for this performance to experience how the coloratura soprano writes poetry in flight.

PROGRAMME

  • Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart Exsultate, jubilate, K. 165
  • Franz Schubert Lachen und Weinen; Suleika II
  • Richard Strauss Amor
  • Frédéric Chopin Nocturne in C minor, Op. 48, No. 1 (Piano Solo)
  • Traditional Chinese Folk Song Jasmine Flower (茉莉花)
  • Gaetano Donizetti "Ah! tardai troppo... O luce di quest'anima" from Linda di Chamounix
  • Eva Dell'Acqua Villanelle
