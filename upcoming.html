Introduction
  • Voices in Flight

Voices in Flight is a journey into the air, carried by the human voice. In the world of vocal music, the coloratura soprano is the voice closest to flight. This sound shimmers and soars beyond the limits of range, unfolding brilliant cascades of notes high above. The agile runs, the crystalline high tones—these are not merely feats of virtuosity, but expressions of emotional freedom. Join in this concert to enjoy how the coloratura soprano writes poetry in flight.
